About Me

Meet arvdevable, a young and talented music producer from Magelang, Central Java, Indonesia. At just 13, he's already made a name for himself in the electronic music scene with his unique sound and thought-provoking lyrics.

He began experimenting with music production at 11 and quickly found a passion for creating electronic songs. He draws inspiration from his love of writing short stories and novels, often incorporating themes of loneliness, self-examination, and the contrast between chaos and beauty into his music.

His latest album, "lifelike," is his biggest yet and showcases his diverse range of skills and emotions. The tracks are a rollercoaster of emotions, with a mix of instruments and styles that will keep you engaged and captivated. From mellow and introspective to energetic and powerful, arvdevable's music is a dynamic and evolving listening experience that is sure to inspire.

arvdevable is a rising star in the Indonesian music scene and is sure to make waves in the international electronic music scene as well. Keep an eye out for his latest releases and live performances as he continues to push the boundaries of electronic music and storytelling.
